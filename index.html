The NeuroBridge Initiative

NEUROBRIDGE is a transnational educational program funded by Italy's PNRR and led by five Italian universities. It provides international students and researchers with advanced training, research mobility opportunities, and workshops in interdisciplinary neuroscience and neurotechnology.
